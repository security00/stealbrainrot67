So, if you’re looking to grab the 67 Brainrot in Steal a Brainrot, here’s all you need to know, as well as how much money you can make from it. It’s worth noting that you can buy Admin Lucky Blocks for $100 million a piece, so if you’re looking for 67, you’ll need to save up for the next Admin Abuse .
