The 67 is a Secret-tier unit in Steal a Brainrot released on September 13, 2025, with the Extinct Event. Based on the popular 6-7 meme that went viral on TikTok and other social media platforms in early 2025, the brainrot looks like a humanoid version of the digits 6 and 7 in the game. How To Obtain the Secret 67 Brainrot

As of the current Steal a Brainrot patch, you can only get the 67 from the Admin Lucky Block. Every time you open one, you have a 0.25% chance to get the unit. This makes 67 the rarest brainrot you can get from the Admin Lucky Block. What’s more, the Admin Lucky Block is an Admin-tier item in the game, making it extremely difficult to find. It only spawns during Admin events, such as the Taco Tuesdays or Admin Abuse events, before major updates.

If an admin spawns the Admin Lucky Block, you have to spend $100 million to buy it. Unlike other blocks, it does open instantaneously, and you do not have to wait to learn what brainrot you get.
