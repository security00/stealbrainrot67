How to find Steal a Brainrot 67 & drop rate

byJessica Filby
Updated: Nov 04, 2025, 14:37
Dexerto

The 67 Brainrot in Roblox’s Steal a Brainrot can make you rich, if you’re lucky enough to find it and claim it for yourself.

Steal a Brainrot players have been hunting for one extremely valuable Brainrot for months now, following its addition back in the game’s September update, but this mysterious item is far from simple to unlock, despite the immense income it’ll bring you.

So, if you’re looking to grab the 67 Brainrot in Steal a Brainrot, here’s all you need to know, as well as how much money you can make from it.

Article continues after ad

How to get 67 in Steal a Brainrot

67 is a Secret Brainrot you can only get in Admin Lucky Blocks, which typically spawn during Admin Abuse events.

It’s worth noting that you can buy Admin Lucky Blocks for $100 million a piece, so if you’re looking for 67, you’ll need to save up for the next Admin Abuse.

Article continues after ad

If you grab an Admin Lucky Block, you’ll have the chance to receive 67, or the following Brainrots:

  • Guerriro Digitale
  • La Grande Combinasion
  • Los Spyderinis
  • Blackhole Goat
  • Crabbo Limonetta
  • Alessio
  • Los Bombinitos
  • Carloo

Income rate & drop rate

If you are lucky enough to get hold of the 67 Brainrot, it’ll provide the following stats:

Article continues after ad
  • Income Rate: $7.5m per second
  • Drop Rate: 0.25%
  • Cost: $1.2 billion

So, it’s easy to see why 67 is as strong and sought after as it is. If you manage to get one, you’ll be rolling in the profits; just make sure it doesn’t get stolen.

That’s all you need to know about how to get 67 in Roblox’s Steal a Brainrot. While saving up or waiting for the Admin Abuse, be sure to check out when the next Taco Tuesday (and Admin Abuse) is, or find out how to get free rewards in other games like Blox Fruits, Grow a Garden, or Bee Swarm Simulator.
